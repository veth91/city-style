    Our Story

    Our Story

    Welcome to my little chic boutique.

    I am Carrie Vestrand, fashionista/shoe aficionado, and along with my husband, Tim, I opened City Style in 2007.

    I spent my childhood summers in Detroit, and grew to love all the city had to offer — especially in terms of fashion. The comradery of the city, with the gorgeous old buildings and urban landscapes, created a rich sense of style that stayed hot even in the midst of winter. Style became my hobby, my passion (okay, obsession) and I decided I wanted to share that with the world.

    With Tim's carpentry skills and my expertise with purses, handbags and accessories the idea of City Style became a reality. We decided on Berkley — because, while it has a fantastic, quaint downtown, we thought it needed a little bit of fashion.

    Now, ten years later we're still that burst of fashion in a growing city: a Mom & Pop shop that caters to our customer’s footwear and style needs.

    Need an outfit to ring in your 50th birthday? How about a killer pair of heels for that board meeting? I am excited to work with you until you satisfy your inner fashionista.

    Party Policy/Guidelines

    • City Style provides light refreshments
    • Hostess arrives approximately 10 minutes prior to party
    • Party guests receive 10% discount on full-priced merchandise (does not apply to fund raiser parties)
    • Hostess receives 10% store credit of total party sales to be used toward City Style merchandise. Store Credit is good the evening of the party. (Does not apply to charitable fund raiser parties. A City Style donation will be made based on party sales.)
    • For parties where total sales are $600 or more, Hostess receives a City Pass worth 20% off all future purchases (full-priced items) for one year!
    • Hostess purchases are Final Sale
    • All Shoe Party returns are store credit only
    • Layaways started the evening of the Shoe Party are not valid toward Hostess Shoe Party Totals
